The first task in our Bootcamp journey!

Occupation - Unemployed...it sucks.

I studied Film at university where I specialised in cinematography and editing.
After graduating, I moved to Southwest Japan where I taught English for two years.

Over the last 2 years, I have developed an interest “Machinima”, a term used to describe making cinematic
videos within game engines. It is through this interest where I have developed an interest in
coding. This is due to having to use certain codes and scripts to make the game engine behave in certain
ways. An example of this would be making a helicopter land in a certain position or exploding at a certain time.

Creating films or cinematic cutscenes in this way is incredibly time-consuming and moving forward,
I would like to work and improve on coding skills that will hopefully lead to self-improvement and employment.
