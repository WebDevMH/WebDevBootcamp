About Me

Passionate traveler and and learner of Japanese language and culture. I have enjoyed travelling and exploring Japan's island of Kyushu where I developed my photography skill including that of Drone Photography capturing active volcanoes, waterfalls and wild horses. With music being another passion, I have enjoyed playing guitar and piano over many years and have developed an excellent musical ear for instant, highly accurate improvisation. Lately, I have enjoyed learning 3D animation using Autodesk software, including 3dsmax and MotionBuilder utilising motion capture and game-engines to create a highly favorable short animated film.
